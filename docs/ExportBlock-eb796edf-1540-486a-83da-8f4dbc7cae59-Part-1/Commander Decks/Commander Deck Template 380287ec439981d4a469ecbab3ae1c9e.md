# Commander Deck Template

## Strategy & Playstyle

Describe the deck's primary game plan, secondary lines, and explicit win conditions. Reference the commander's role in the strategy.

## Key Synergy Lines

Document combo lines and engines with step-by-step descriptions of how each works. Only include combos where all pieces are in the deck.

## Strengths & Weaknesses

Honest assessment — what the deck does well and what card types or strategies it struggles against. Include at least 3 of each.

## Matchup Notes

Identify threats that shut the deck down and describe how to play around them. Include favorable matchups and table politics considerations.

## Legality Summary

Total card count, banned card check, color identity verification. This section is computed from deck data.

## Category Breakdown

Functional roles with target slot counts and current actual counts from deck data. Include: Ramp, Draw, Removal, Interaction, Finishers, Lands, and deck-specific categories.

## Upgrade Strategy

Prioritised cards to add and cut, with price tier and ownership status from your collection. Pair adds with suggested cuts.
